Lost Forest

The Lost Forest is an immersive exhibit that transports you to the heart of a tropical rainforest, showcasing a diverse range of plants and animals.

Highlights include:

  • Guided tours every hour
  • Interactive displays about rainforest conservation
  • Special feeding sessions for exotic birds at 10h00am and 14h00pm

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest is located in the center of the zoo, easily accessible from all main paths.

ANIMALS MAP PLACES

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth.

They are the gentle giants of the jungle

Gemsbok

Gemsbok are antelope with long, straight horns, thriving in arid regions.

Monkey

Monkeys are agile, intelligent, and playful, thriving in diverse environments.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks, spotted coats, and graceful movements.

Gorilla

Gorillas are the largest primates, known for their strength and intelligence.

Koala

tree-dwelling marsupial from Australia, known for its fluffy ears, eucalyptus diet, and sleepy lifestyle.

Lion

fierce and majestic predator, ruling the African plains with strength and pride

Panda

gentle and adorable bear, native to the bamboo forests of China

Tiger

powerful, striped big cat and apex predator, roaming forests and grasslands across Asia

Warthog

tough, tusked wild pig of the African savanna, known for its warty face and quirky charm.

Polar Bear

majestic, white-furred bear of the Arctic, known for its strength and swimming ability.

Kangaroo

iconic marsupial of Australia, known for its powerful hind legs and unique hopping movement.

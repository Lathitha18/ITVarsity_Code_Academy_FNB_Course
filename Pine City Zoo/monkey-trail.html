ANIMALS MAP PLACES

Monkey Trail

Explore the lush canopy of the Monkey Trail, where you can observe a variety of primates in their naturalistic habitats.

What you’ll encounter:

  • 8h00am : Guided tour through the trail with a primate specialist
  • 10h30am : Feeding time observation—watch how different species interact with their food
  • 13h00pm : Educational talk on monkey behavior, communication, and conservation

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is located on the eastern side of the zoo, near the riverbank and just past the Lost Forest.
